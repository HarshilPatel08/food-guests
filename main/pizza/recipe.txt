1. Make Dough: Mix flour, yeast, salt, sugar, water, and oil. Knead and let rise.
2. Preheat Oven: Set to 475°F (245°C).
3. Roll Dough: Roll out dough into a circle.
4. Add Sauce: Spread tomato sauce on the dough.
5. Add Toppings: Cheese, veggies, meats, etc.
6. Bake: 12-15 minutes until golden.
7. Serve: Slice and enjoy!
